About Brainiacs

Where Passion Meets
Professional Teaching

Brainiacs International Montessori and Teacher Training Institute is a unit of Brainiacs Institution, based in Cuddalore, Tamil Nadu. Founded on a single conviction: that the quality of early childhood education is determined entirely by those who guide it.

Our programmes are certified and approved by central government bodies, globally recognised, and backed by a 100% job placement assistance programme. We deliver offline, hands-on training at our campus in Gandhi Nagar, Semmandalam, Cuddalore.
